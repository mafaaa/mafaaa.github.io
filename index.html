About

Logo & Description

Our logo basically consists of our business name, the mascot face, and the stars. Of course, the name says that our patty is what makes us different and special than other burger shops out there. Then we have our mascot, the face with white mustache. Through and through we will be putting that mascot everywhere including our menu, sign, and promotions. The star represents our business as a symbol of excellence. Last but not least, the color brown is a warm color that stimulates the appetites. while it is sometimes considered dull, it also represents steadfastness, simplicity, friendliness, dependability, and health.

Our Signatures



Nemo Fillet












Only the tastiest Dory is worthy of being in our Dory Fillet. Cooked and topped with fresh lettuce and tomato and laid on a bed of diced onion and sliced pickle, the Dory Fillet is a great escape from the ordinary. Two creamy layers of tartar sauce make it even more delicious.




Mini Tower













Is bigger better? You get to decide with our popular Mini Tower. It's two thick and tasty 150g patties held together with cheese, butter lettuce, tomato and topped with a fried egg. Held together by a freshly toasted bun.




Lamb Supreme













150g of juicy lamb, cooked and seasoned to perfection. Topped with crisp lettuce, fresh tomato and a slice of mouth-watering cheese. Add in a base of mayo, diced onion and sliced pickles, served on a warm toasted bun.
