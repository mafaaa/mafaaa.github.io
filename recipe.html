Recipe


CheeseBurger

Take one 80g patty of pure Australian beef, season it and cook it to perfection. Place it on a toasted bun and a bed of diced onions and sliced pickles. Top it with cheese, fresh lettuce and tomato and you'll see why it's so popular.

Original Smokin'

When you're the original, everything else is just a copy. We take 150g of the finest Aussie beef, season and cook it to perfection - top it with fresh lettuce and tomato brought in from the Cameron Highlands and you've got yourself the burger that all other burgers envy.

Classic Chic' Sandwich

Delicious. Timeless. 80g of tender chicken cooked to perfection and topped with cheese, fresh lettuce and tomato. Perfect for any appetite.

Nemo Fillet

Only the tastiest Fish is worthy of being in our Nemo Fillet. Cooked and topped with fresh lettuce and tomato and laid on a bed of diced onion and sliced pickle, the Nemo Fillet is a great escape from the ordinary. Two creamy layers of tartar sauce make it even more delicious.

Hawaii Chicken

Surf's up with our Hawaii Chicken. A tasty, seasoned 150g chicken patty on a bed of mayo and topped with only the freshest lettuce and tomato. Your taste buds are sure to catch a wave with this one.

Mini Tower

Is bigger better? You get to decide with our popular Mini Tower. It's two thick and tasty 150g patties held together with cheese, butter lettuce, tomato and topped with a fried egg. Held together by a freshly toasted bun.

Lamb Supreme

150g of juicy lamb, cooked and seasoned to perfection. Topped with crisp lettuce, fresh tomato and a slice of mouth-watering cheese. Add in a base of mayo, diced onion and sliced pickles, served on a warm toasted bun.

BlueCheese Burger

A beautifully seasoned 150g patty dripping with creamy blue cheese topped with fresh butter lettuce and tomato and laid on a bed of diced onions and sliced pickle. Place it all between a freshly toasted bun and you've got a winner.
